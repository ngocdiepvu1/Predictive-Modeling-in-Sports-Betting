Notes for Football Data

All data is either in csv or xlsx format

Key to results data:
Date
Time

Div = League Division
HomeTeam = Home Team
AwayTeam = Away Team
FTHG = Full Time Home Team Goals
FTAG = Full Time Away Team Goals
FTR = Full Time Result (0=Home Win, 1=Draw, 2=Away Win)
HTHG = Half Time Home Team Goals
HTAG = Half Time Away Team Goals
HTR = Half Time Result (0=Home Win, 1=Draw, 2=Away Win)
HS = Home Team Shots
AS = Away Team Shots
HST = Home Team Shots on Target
AST = Away Team Shots on Target


Key to 1X2 (match) betting odds data:

AvgH = Market average home win odds
AvgD = Market average draw win odds
AvgA = Market average away win odds


Key to total goals betting odds:


Avg>2.5 = Market average over 2.5 goals
Avg<2.5 = Market average under 2.5 goals


Key to Asian handicap betting odds:

AHh = Market size of handicap (home team)



Closing odds (last odds before match starts):

AvgCH = Market average closing home win odds
AvgCD = Market average closing draw win odds
AvgCA = Market average closing away win odds

AvgC>2.5 = Market closing average over 2.5 goals
AvgC<2.5 = Market closing average under 2.5 goals

AHCh = Closing market size of handicap (home team)

HLP = Home Leaugue Place
ALP = Away Leaugue Place
